博物馆咨询场景对话

Receptionist

Good afternoon. How can I help you?

接待员：下午好。我能帮您什么？

Jason

Could you tell me where the Silk Road exhibition is?

杰森：您能告诉我丝绸之路展览在哪里吗？

Receptionist

It's in Gallery N1. Here's a map of the museum to help you find it.

接待员：在 N1 展厅。这是博物馆的地图，帮您找地方用的。

Jason

Oh, thanks. Can I take photos in the exhibition?

杰森：哦，谢谢。我能在展览里拍照吗？

Receptionist

You can take photos, but you can't use the flash.

接待员：您可以拍照，但不能用闪光灯。

Jason

I see. Are there any guided tours for the exhibition?

杰森：我明白了。展览有讲解导览吗？

Receptionist

There's a guided tour every hour. You can get the information from the information board at the entrance of the gallery.

接待员：每小时有一场讲解导览。您可以在展厅入口的信息公告牌上看详情。

Jason

Great. Thank you so much.

杰森：好的。非常感谢您。

Receptionist

You're welcome. Enjoy your visit!

接待员：不客气。祝您参观愉快！

朋友间对话场景

Lily

Wow! What a nice dress! Where did you buy it?

莉莉：哇！好漂亮的裙子！你在哪买的？

Emma

Oh, I didn't need to buy it. It was made by my granny last year.

艾玛：哦，我没买。这是我奶奶去年做的。

Lily

Your granny? Is she a tailor?

莉莉：你奶奶？她是裁缝吗？

Emma

No, she isn't, but her grandfather was a tailor. She learned the skills from him.

艾玛：不是，但她爷爷是裁缝。她跟爷爷学的手艺。

Lily

The dress is well - designed and quite unique. It is a masterpiece.

莉莉：这裙子设计得很好，还特别独特，简直是件杰作。

Emma

Yes, I admire my granny so much.

艾玛：对呀，我可崇拜我奶奶了。

Lily

What is she like?

莉莉：她是个什么样的人呀？

Emma

She is a person who is persistent and focuses on details.

艾玛：她是个很执着、注重细节的人。

Lily

And I think she must be very patience. It needs time to make a dress like that.

莉莉：而且我觉得她肯定很有耐心，做这样的裙子得花时间呢。

Emma

She spent 2 weeks making it.

艾玛：她做这裙子花了两周时间。
